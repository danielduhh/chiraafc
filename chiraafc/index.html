By 2018, we plan to bring Chiraa FC
from Ghana to the Schwans USA Cup.

Through our soccer club, we hope to assist our students with school supplies, lunch meal programs, and tuition assistance. We hope to instill the ideas of community service, personal responsibility, empowerment, good character, pride, & the value of education. We want the kids to learn that lending a hand only requires reaching out. We hope that through this we could someday help send each and every participant on to higher learning & in turn, they would someday return and lend a helping hand to the next generation.

Community

Established July 2015, Chiraa FC Foundation now has soccer clubs in Chiraa & Kwatire.

Empowerment

Each team is equipped with jerseys, balls, bibs, nets, cones, & a set Kits.

Education

We've provided Kwame Mensah with registration & school fees, and hope to provide more!
